What's your favorite location?

Especially during the outbreak of Coronavirus, I think we all find ourselves thinking about all of our favorite places around the world that we wish we could visit. Because of this, I wanted to create a space on my website where anyone and everyone can post a picture of their favorite locations in the world. This collective image gallery of some of the special locations around the world is a reminder of all of the normal, the extraordinary, and everything in between that is special to us. I encourage you to post a picture of your favorite location, be it near or far, and to reflect on what you value in the places that are most special to you.
