About my hobbies

Growing up, I developed a deep interest in mathematics and music. I love looking back at some of my past school projects from third grade or so, since they remind me that - with all the changes that have taken place in my life - there has been something in constant bloom and growth about my passions and interests.

Later on, in high school, I developed a passion for Computer Science as well. However, this came at little surprise ot me when I looked back at my childhood days of solving logic puzzles and math games. Computer Science, to me, is one giant logic puzzle with problems that I have yet to solve. That's one of the reasons I love it.

Outside of math and CS, I have continued my musical studies, and I try to have fun while doing so! I play music with friends and I don't limit myself to any particular genre.

Hiking, biking, and more!

I've always loved nature. I'm really not the most athletic of people, but I try to get outdoors sometimes to exercise or more generally to just appreciate nature.

Recently, I've been going on bikes or hikes with my family and girlfriend. It's a great way to spend time outside while remaining socially distant. I live close to NY, where there a lot of expansive forests that are wonderful for hiking. If I go at the right hour, sometimes I don't see a single other person. However, I do see a lot of wildlife, such as clusters of deer. It's so fun!
